Custom Exhibits

If you need help with a custom software or exhibit installation project, we are here to help.

Along with developing a world class HCI framework and cutting-edge, spin-off products such as GestureWorks Gameplay, we take on select custom products on a contract basis. We’ve worked with world famous museums and Fortune 500 companies.

Ideum, the creator of GestureWorks, intimately understands interactive multitouch, motion gesture recognition and other emerging technologies. We have years of experience in developing applications with our clients. In addition to creating custom software, we have expertise in hardware development that few software and design companies can match – we design and build multitouch tables, touch walls, and custom exhibit installations. Ideum also rents multitouch tables and coffee tables for traveling exhibits, special events, and trade shows.

We work in aluminum, glass, wood and other high-quality materials to create beautiful and long-lasting exhibits. We’ve developed exhibits in aluminum with a variety of custom powder-coated colors and textures. We have worked with etched glass and custom-etched aluminum. We have even created realistic wood grains on aluminum through hydro-dipping.
