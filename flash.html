GestureWorks Flash

Hello!

Thank you for your interest in GestureWorks Flash. We have exciting news to share! GestureWorks Flash has merged with Open Exhibits. Open Exhibits 4.1 offers, for free, all of the features of the final available version of GestureWorks Flash.

 

Open Exhibits is free for museums, educators, non-profits and commercial developers (there are however some restrictions for commercial use, please see the legal page on Open Exhibits for details). Visit the Open Exhibits website for more information and to download a free copy.

GestureWorks Flash Pro users can still access personal and rapid ticketed support here. This support will be available for one year from the date of purchase of your Pro license (as stated in the original software license agreement).

We hope you are excited to see that Open Exhibits has all of the features of GestureWorks Flash and a larger and active user community. Support for Open Exhibits can be found in the Open Exhibits forum. GestureWorks Flash standard license users can also direct their questions to the Open Exhibits forum.

Thanks again for your interest and support.

- The Open Exhibits & GestureWorks Team
